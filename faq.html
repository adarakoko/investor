Deposits

On our investment platform, we exclusively accept cryptocurrency(BITCOIN & USDT TRC20) payments to offer a secure, transparent, and efficient transaction process. Cryptocurrencies eliminate the need for intermediaries, reduce transaction fees, and provide faster processing times compared to traditional payment methods. Additionally, the decentralized nature of crypto ensures greater privacy and security for our investors. By embracing this innovative payment system, we aim to streamline investments and offer a modern, future-focused experience.

When making a deposit, you'll need to follow the platform's guidelines regarding minimum amounts, accepted payment methods, and the timeline for deposit processing. It's essential to confirm that your deposit has been successfully credited to your account, as this enables you to participate in investment opportunities.

Withdrawals

Withdrawals from our real estate investment platform are designed to be straightforward and secure. To withdraw funds, simply follow the platform's withdrawal process, which includes selecting your preferred withdrawal method and verifying your identity

You can withdraw your earnings from our real estate investment platform once they have been credited to your account. The timing of this credit depends on the specific investment type and the agreed-upon terms, such as rental income distribution or property sale profits. Typically, earnings are available for withdrawal shortly after these events are finalized. Always refer to your investment agreement for detailed timelines, and feel free to contact our support team with any questions.
